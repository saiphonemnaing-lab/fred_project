FRED_PROJECT is a data analysis project integrated with prediction mechanism, using statistical data science techniques.
Data included: \n
1. cpi = Sticky-Price "Consumer Price Index less Food and Energy (percent change from year ago; seasonally adjusted; monthly)" \n
2. gdp = GDP "Gross Domestic Product (billions of dollars; seasonally adjusted annual rate; quarterly)"
3. rgdp = Real-GDP "Real Gross Domestic Product (billions of chained 2017 dollars; seasonally adjusted annual rate; quarterly)"
4. unrate = Unemployment Rate "Unemployment Rate (percent; seasonally adjusted; monthly)"
5. cd = Bank Interest Rates "3-Month or 90-Day Rates and Yields: Certificates of Deposit: Total for United States (percent; not seasonally adjusted; monthly)"
